3. Off-Campus Thesis

Thesis research is usually carried out in laboratories operated by I.A.A.U. and located on-campus. There are some exceptions to this general rule which do not require explicit approval:

Students in the Internship Program who are doing their theses at the company need only arrange for the Company Letters (see Appendix A and Appendix B) to be received with their proposals and final documents.

All such theses must have an I.A.A.U. faculty or approved research staff supervisor as well as a supervisor at the off-campus location.

Students not covered by the cases listed above must request permission of the Department Officer to pursue thesis research off-campus. Under no circumstances will projects undertaken at a company be approved as Degree of M.Eng. theses after the fact.
