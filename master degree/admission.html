List of documents required for admission

DEADLINE

  • Documents for admission to the Master's program are accepted until September 30, 17 at the International University of Alatoo,
    at the address: Bishkek, Tunguch microdistrict, Ankara str., 1/8
    Telephones for information: 0312 54-92-38, 0312 63-14-25

    • Note for international students: Note to foreign citizens Translation of the passport, diploma and its insert into Kyrgyz or Russian (the translation must be confirmed by a certified notary or translation company).
    Requirements on the specialty:

      Bachelor's degree or computer scientist with a 75% performance over the past two years. A bachelor's degree or a technical degree is also accepted with an academic achievement of 85% in subjects related to computer science over the last two years of training. These competences must be documented. Professional programming activities can be recognized as fulfilling the criterion for admission. If necessary, the student must listen to the courses on in-depth programming and software development in the Bachelor's degree program of KGPPI or Alatoo University. These compulsory courses are read not in the evening or on the weekends. For possible courses, a fee is charged in addition to the principal amount of the tuition fee.

    Requirements for foreign languages:
    • English B2 or

    • English B1 or German B2

    • The level of proficiency in English is determined by the teachers of the University of Alatoo.

    • The level of knowledge of the German language is determined by the teachers of KGPPI KGUSTA

    Documents for admission to the master's degree

    • Application

    • The original of the bachelor's degree (specialist) with the application of the statement from the credit record

    • 2 photos of 3x4

    • Copy of passport (original for viewing)

    • Agreement

    • Personal account for personnel accounting
