Competition for the support of graduate students, applicants and PhD-doctoral students

Within the framework of the DAAD project "Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics", implemented between 2016 and 2019, a competition is announced among teachers and young scientists of the International University Alatoo and KSUSTA for research in the Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics and the Western Saxon University Zwickau (WHZ).

Aim

The involvement of scientific research in training and the strengthening of faculty at KGPPI. Criterion for participation in the program is the relevance of the thesis topic. In addition, the defense of the candidate's thesis is planned no later than two years. Research and training are carried out in KGPPI. The candidate undertakes to teach and conduct research at KGPPI for at least three years.

Spheres of research:

The project supports research activities in the following areas:

• Information Security

• Applied Informatics - Mobile Systems

The management of the research topics is conducted by the dean of the Faculty of CGAPI, Dr. Abdulaev AA.

Support Form:

• Fee for conducting research in KGPPI in the amount of 250,00 € per month. At the same time, 50% of the workload must be allocated for scientific research;

• Scientific internship with a duration of up to 3 months at the University of Zwickau. Travel expenses up to 700 € are paid from the project and a monthly stipend of 1.000 € is provided. Of this amount, the same must be purchased health insurance in Germany.

Required documents (in English or German)

  • Completed application form for participation;

  • A detailed written presentation (Exposè) on the topic of dissertation work

  • A letter of recommendation from the chief executive;

  • Summary;

  • Certificate certifying knowledge of German or English at level B1.

Selection committee:

  • Dean of KGPPI, Dr. Abdulaev A.A.

  • project manager DAAD.

Application deadline:

  • no later than August 31, 2017;

  • send to kgfi.project@gmail.com
