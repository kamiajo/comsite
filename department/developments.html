Obstacle avoidance robot competition 2018

With the support of people interested in promoting robotics, April 26, 2016. the teaching staff of the Department of Industrial Electronics of Atatürk-Alatoo International University conducted a "Contest of Robots", the main condition of which was the passage of a certain route, for the shortest time, while avoiding obstacles in its path. The aim of the competition was to attract young minds to the fascinating world of electronics and robotics.

The participants of the event were students of the International Atatürk-Alatoo University, as well as students of the high school "Cambridge Silk Road International School". In total, 10 teams from students and 3 teams of students were declared.   Participants, who presented their robots with a group of no more than three people, passed mandatory registration. After that, solemn opening ceremony took place, in which Bedelbai Mamadiev, dean of the Faculty of New Technologies of the International University of Ataturk-Alatoo, spoke. Also, the honorable guests were the founders of the most popular online store in Bishkek, "geek.kg". The distance of the first round of the contest was 15 meters. The obstacle course on the path of the robots included wooden obstacles, chaotically located along the entire path of the participants. Accompanying the passage of the route could be one member of the claimed team. The first round winners were the participants from the Moun-Tech team, who passed the entire distance in 1min.38 seconds.

As the competition progressed, the competition attracted more and more interest among students. Also, the contest did not leave spectators and teachers from other universities unminded, who became ardent supporters of the teams. The second round was declared a distance of 10 meters, no less complicated and confusing to pass. Excellent competition was made by the group "Moun-Tech", the team "Iceronic".  At the end of two intense rounds, after a small referee vote, the winners were announced. They were the team "Moun-Tech", awarded an honorary certificate and a cash prize. The second group was "Iceronic", the guys were not upset, because they were also awarded a certificate and a prize. The third, but not the worst, became "Wally".   All other contestants were also awarded certificates of participants.
