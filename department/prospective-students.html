Orientation day 2017

September 4, 2017 International University Alatoo opened its doors for new students, as well as for teachers and senior students. For first-year students this was a particularly important day, because Entry into the university means entering into adulthood. And to help them adapt, the university organized an Orientation Day, where they could get to know the university closer.

The official part of the program began with the welcoming words of the rector of the MUA-Sanjarbek Erdolatov, and the special guest of the JIA Business Association Chairman Temir Azhikulov congratulated the students on the opening of the new academic year. The concert part of the program was accompanied by songs of the famous singer of Kyrgyzstan Mirbek Atabekov. Well, further, our students showed their talents with songs and jokes no worse than others.

A free lunch for students was given in honor of the holiday. And at the end of the program, the university's administrative staff gave useful information about the OCS online system, about student clubs, about the centers of medicine and psychology, and about the rules of etiquette. Well, at the very end, I want to wish everyone to achieve high goals, fruitful work and good relations with others!
