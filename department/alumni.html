Alumni meeting

By tradition, each year in August, the Ala-Too International University invites its graduates to the campus of the university and gives them the opportunity to recall past student years. Without violating this tradition, this year the university also invited former students to a dinner party.

Graduates, having tasted the meals of the student canteen, returned for a while at a time when long queues passed and bought lunch on trays. Seeing their teachers, the former students remembered how they had passed classes and waited for breaks. And the teachers, in turn, were happy to talk with their former students and could not rejoice at the success of their students.

AIU rector Sanzharbek Erdolatov greeted all and thanked the graduates that to this day they do not forget about their university. Yesterday's students today are already loving parents, public figures, working for the good of the country, specialists in state institutions.

Graduates, in turn, expressed gratitude to the teachers for their tireless work and patience, and offered on behalf of the graduates to contribute to the development of the university.

And in the end, this good tradition of the meeting of the graduates of the AIU was completed with a general photo for memory.
